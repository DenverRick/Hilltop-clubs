# Leader outreach email — draft for Dayna's review

Send one per leader. Personalize `[Leader First Name]` and `[Club Name]`. Everything else stays the same.

**Subject:** Opt your club into the new Hilltop Clubs Directory?

Hi [Leader First Name],

I'm Rick Miles — fellow Hilltop resident, leader of the Senior Geeks Club. With Dayna Grober's blessing, I've built a small searchable directory of our resident clubs (sister project to the Pickleball site some of you have seen):

**https://hilltop-clubs.netlify.app**

Take a look — it's quick to browse. I'd love to include **[Club Name]**, but the directory is **opt-in**: your club won't appear unless you say yes.

Here's what would show up on your club's page:

- Club name, category, leader name(s)
- The blurb already on file from Dayna's 2026 Club List
- Meeting day / time / location (where it's known)
- TeamReach code (where it's listed)
- Optional, if you want them later: a longer description, a banner photo, YouTube videos — you control these via a simple form I'll send you

**What stays private:** your email address never appears on the public site. When a resident clicks "I'm interested," the page hands their own mail app a pre-filled message addressed to you — so the email itself is what makes the introduction. Your address is never published, scraped, or visible to a casual visitor.

To opt in, just reply **YES** to this email. I'll activate your listing right away. If you'd rather skip the directory, no need to reply — and no hard feelings. Same goes if you want to opt in later; you can do that any time.

Happy to answer any questions.

Thanks,
Rick Miles
Senior Geeks Club
hilltopseniorgeeks@gmail.com
