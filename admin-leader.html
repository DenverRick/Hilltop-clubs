Pick your club and enter the email address management has on file for you, then click Log in. Once we confirm it's you, the edit fields appear — pre-filled with what's currently on your page, so you only change what you want. Only your own club can be edited.

Note: leaving a field blank keeps the existing value (it doesn't erase it). If you actually want to clear a field, email Rick.

How your email is handled: it's never shown on your public club page or in any listing. Residents reach you through an "I'm interested" button that opens a message in their own mail app — so your address appears only in the email they send to you, never displayed on the site itself.

How do I get an App Password?
  1. Sign in to your club's Gmail, then turn on 2-Step Verification (Google Account → Security).
  2. Open myaccount.google.com/apppasswords, create one named "RSVP", and copy the 16-character code.
  3. Paste it above and save. We store it encrypted — never in plain text.
